Frequently Asked

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I create a new group?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, select "New Group," give it a name, and invite your friends via link, contact list, or QR code. It's that simple!

Can I use Talangin offline?

Yes! You can add and view expenses offline. They'll automatically sync with your group members once you're back online.

How does the OCR receipt scanner work?

Simply take a photo of your receipt. Our AI-powered OCR will automatically extract merchant name, items, prices, and total. You can review and edit before saving.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all your data. Your financial information is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.
